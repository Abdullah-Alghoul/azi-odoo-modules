Sales Team Industries

This module was written to extend the functionality of Sales Teams and Sales Regions to support industries and allow you to assign multiple industries to a team and an industry to a customer.

Usage

Create partner industries. Assign industries to sales teams and an industry to a customer.

For further information, please visit:
