Sales Team Auto Assign

This module was written to extend the functionality of Sales Teams, Sales Team Industries and Sales Regions to allow you to assign sales teams to a customer based on sales regions and industries.

Usage

Automatically assign new customers to sales teams given a state and industry(optional).
