Sales Team Regions

This module was written to extend the functionality of Sales Teams to support sales regions which allow you to assign a sales region to a sales team and sales teams to a customer.

Usage

Define sales team regions (from country groups, countries, and/or states) per sales team. Assign sales teams to customers.

For further information, please visit:
